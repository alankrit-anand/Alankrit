Alankrit Anand is currently a B.Tech. computer science undergraduate student in Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. He also works in National Service Scheme of IITR in Exam Education cell to provide opportunities to economically and socially deprived children to crack JEE and other engineering entrance examinations.

Born in Patna, Bihar, Alankrit is enthusiastic towards science and technology. He received secondary and higher education at D.A.V. Public School B.S.E.B. Colony where he gained a lot of knowledge and creativity. He qualified NTSE in class 10. After joining Fiitjee Patna in class 11, his journey of IIT-JEE began. He participated in various olympiads of physics and mathematics where he got decent rank. Participated in KVPY SX in class 12 and got qualified in the written and HR interview with flying colours.
